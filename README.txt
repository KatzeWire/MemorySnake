My alteration of the snake game is called 'Memory Snake'!!!

Instructions:
Food will turn invisible after a set amount of time.
Blue pellets (NORMAL FOOD): size+1 & score+1
Green pellets (ROTTEN FOOD): size-1 & score-1
Orange pellets (RIPE FOOD): size+0 & score+5
Purple pellets (REVEAL): reveals normal and rotten food
Oh, and use arrow keys to move!
